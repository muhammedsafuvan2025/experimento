LENS
05 — lens & travel

Frame by Frame

I want to film in every country on earth. That's not a hobby — that's a life plan.

Why Every Country

Most people travel to relax. I travel to record. Every new country is a different angle on the same human story — different language, same fears, same love, same hunger for meaning. I want to capture that. All of it. With a camera and enough time.

I moved from Kochi, India to London, Ontario — two worlds apart. That move alone is a documentary I haven't made yet. The plan is to eventually make it. Along with the 193 others.

Geopolitics

Borders are stories we tell with force. I read everything — Kissinger, Mearsheimer, Peter Zeihan, The Economist. The world is a chessboard and most people don't even know they're pieces. Understanding power structures isn't pessimism — it's literacy.

Space Exploration

We are at the most important moment in human history for becoming multi-planetary. SpaceX. Artemis. Webb telescope images. The universe is 93 billion light-years wide and we're still arguing about borders on a pale blue dot.

Technology & AI

I'm in IT because technology is the closest thing to magic I can actually understand. AI in 2025 feels like how the internet felt in 1995 — most people don't realize what's coming. I want to be on the right side of that shift.

Philosophy of Mind

Consciousness is the hard problem. What is experience? Why does the universe generate subjective feeling at all? Severance made me think about this for a week. So did 2001. I don't have answers — just better questions every year.
